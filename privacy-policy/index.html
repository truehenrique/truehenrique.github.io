Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how the truehenrique.com blog collects, uses, stores, and protects the information of its users.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in various ways to improve your experience and the content we offer:

  • Navigation Data: We collect information about how you interact with our blog, such as pages visited, time spent, browser type, operating system, and IP address. This is done through analytics tools like Google Analytics.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to store preferences, record specific user information about which pages the user accesses or visits, customize web page content based on visitors' browser type or other information that the visitor sends.
  • Third-Party Information: We may receive information about you from third parties, such as Google AdSense, which uses cookies to display relevant ads based on your previous visits to our or other websites.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Improve and personalize your browsing experience on the blog.
  • Analyze traffic and user behavior to optimize the content and structure of the site.
  • Display relevant ads through Google AdSense.
  • Ensure the security and proper functioning of the blog.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to third parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or providing services to you, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

We may release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety.

4. Google AdSense and DART Cookies

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our blog. The DART cookie enables Google to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Request the correction of any inaccurate data.
  • Request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings. However, this may affect the functionality of some site features.

6. Links to Third-Party Websites

Our blog may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these sites. We recommend that you review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page. We advise you to review this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via email: hrique.work@gmail.com.
